## Failure Mode Safeguards

If PA or CA drops below 0.5 in any condition:

1. **Pause** - The framing may cause harm
2. **Document** - What specifically went wrong?
3. **Adjust** - Add explicit honesty requirements
4. **Re-run** - Test if adjustment works
5. **Report** - Negative results are valuable
